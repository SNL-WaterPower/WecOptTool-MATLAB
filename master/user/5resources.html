5. Resources¶

This page provides additional resources for users of WecOptTool.

5.1. Webinar¶

A one hour webinar [4] was presented on September 29th 2020. This webinar provides interested users with a tutorial and background.

5.2. Additional Reading¶

The following references specifically focusing on WecOptTool are available:

  • Optimal control of wave energy converters [5] - While written years before the development of WecOptTool, Giorgio Bacelli’s doctoral thesis provides much of the framework upon which WecOptTool is based.
  • Initial conceptual demonstration of control co-design for WEC optimization [3] - This paper presents the basic framework of WecOptTool along with a series of examples using the Sandia WaveBot.
